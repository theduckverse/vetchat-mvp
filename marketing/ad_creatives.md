
# Ad & Social Copy Starters

## Short hooks
- “Veteran? Text us for quick, respectful support — non‑clinical, 24/7.”
- “Not medical advice. Real resources. Text HELLO to +1 (xxx) xxx‑xxxx.”

## Primary text (Meta)
Veteran or family member? Get respectful, non‑clinical support over SMS.
We share practical resources (benefits, community, coping tips) and always show
the Veterans Crisis Line when needed. Text HELLO to +1 (xxx) xxx‑xxxx.

## Google RSA headlines
- SMS support for Veterans
- Non‑clinical veteran help
- Quick resources by text

> Always include: Veterans Crisis Line (988 → press 1) or text 838255 in site footer.
